ABOUT RACHEL PARK

Rachel Park is a current second-year undergraduate student studying Computational Media at the Georgia Institute of Technology. Since then, she knew she wanted to be involved in a career that combined both of these passions. Her dream job is to become a UI/UX designer for Apple in the future! For now, she is still building upon her basic understanding of website design.
